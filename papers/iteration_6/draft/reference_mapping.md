## Unsupported or Weakly Supported Claims

All 16 [REF] markers in the original draft have been assigned at least one supporting reference. However, the following claims have relatively weaker direct support:

1. **Estrogen receptor expression in the cornea (Claim 16):** The assigned references (Naderan 2017, Hoogewoud 2013) provide indirect clinical evidence. More direct molecular evidence exists (e.g., Spoerl et al. on porcine corneas) but was not assigned as a separate reference to keep the list focused.

2. **Tetracycline and MMP inhibition in corneal context (Claim 19):** The assigned references support MMP involvement in KC and inflammatory molecule presence in tears. A dedicated study on tetracycline's MMP-inhibitory action specifically in corneal tissue would further strengthen this claim.

3. **Vitamin C in corneal stroma (Claim 22):** Supported by a review of nutritional factors. More specific biochemical studies on ascorbic acid concentration in corneal tissue exist but were not individually cited.
