# Figures

## Figure: Multivariable Odds Ratios of the Association Between Keratoconus and Health History
- **Type**: Forest plot showing odds ratios with 95% confidence intervals
- **Content**: All 11 variables displayed with their multivariable ORs
- **Key features**:
  - Ocular surface disease (OR = 6.04) is the most significant positive association
  - Obesity (OR = 1.82) is the second strongest positive association
  - Estrogen-containing medication (OR = 1.77) is a newly identified positive association
  - Tetracycline (OR = 1.36) is a newly identified positive association
  - Smoking (OR = 0.64) is the only negative association (protective)
  - Reference line at OR = 1.0
- **Note**: Ocular surface disease includes participants with dry eye, allergic or atopic conjunctivitis, or eczema
