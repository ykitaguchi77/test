# Figures

## Figure 1: Kaplan-Meier Survival Curves for Myopia Onset by Referral Compliance Group

Kaplan-Meier survival curves showing the probability of remaining free from myopia over the 6-year follow-up period. Three curves are shown for the Never (blue), Tardily (red), and Timely (green) groups. The Timely group showed the highest survival probability throughout follow-up, while the Tardily group showed the lowest. The curves begin to separate after approximately 2 years of follow-up, with the divergence becoming more pronounced over time. The x-axis shows follow-up time in years (0-6), and the y-axis shows the probability of remaining non-myopic (0-1.0). Number at risk tables are shown below the curves.

**Key data from survival analysis:**
- Median time to myopia onset, Never group: 4.92 years (95% CI: 4.83-4.92)
- Median time to myopia onset, Tardily group: 4.50 years (95% CI: 4.00-4.92)
- Median time to myopia onset, Timely group: 5.83 years (95% CI: 4.42-5.92)
- Log-rank test P < 0.001

## Figure 2: Trajectories of Annual Spherical Equivalent Change by Referral Compliance Group

Line plot showing the trajectories of annual SE change over age (years) for the three referral compliance groups using generalized additive models. The x-axis represents age in years (5-11), and the y-axis represents annual SE change in diopters. The Timely group (green line) showed slower myopic shift compared to the Never group (blue line) and Tardily group (red line). Shaded areas around each line represent 95% confidence intervals. The Tardily group showed the most rapid myopic shift, particularly between ages 7-9. The curves demonstrate that the protective effect of timely referral becomes more apparent with increasing age.

**Key data from SE progression analysis:**
- Never group: annual SE change = -0.06 +/- 0.57D/year
- Tardily group: annual SE change = -0.14 +/- 0.50D/year
- Timely group: annual SE change = 0.07 +/- 0.60D/year
- P = 0.005

## Figure 3: Forest Plot of Subgroup Analyses for Myopia Incidence

Forest plot showing the hazard ratios for myopia incidence comparing Timely vs. Never groups across subgroups (by sex and district). The overall HR was 0.55 (95% CI: 0.33-0.93). Results were consistent across subgroups, with point estimates generally favoring the Timely group. The x-axis represents hazard ratios on a logarithmic scale, with a vertical line at HR=1.0 indicating no difference.

**Key data from subgroup analyses:**
- Overall: HR 0.55 (95% CI: 0.33-0.93)
- Jing'an district: HR 0.48 (95% CI: 0.24-0.97)
- Minhang district: HR 0.62 (95% CI: 0.31-1.24)
- Pudong district: HR 0.58 (95% CI: 0.27-1.25)
- P for interaction (sex): 0.482
- P for interaction (district): 0.712
