## Figure 1: Flowchart for Selecting the Study Subjects

**Caption:** "Fig. 1 Flowchart for selecting the study subjects"

**Type:** Flowchart / CONSORT-style diagram

**Description:** A flowchart depicting the selection process of study participants.

**Flow:**
- 14,572 children aged 4-6 years screened in 2015 (from three districts: Jing'an, Minhang, Pudong)
- --> 5,917 children needed a referral (40.60% failed vision screening)
- --> Exclusion: <2 screenings OR incomplete personal information
- --> 5,511 children included in final cohort (criteria: >=2 vision screenings AND complete personal information; follow-up: January 2015 to December 2020)
  - High-risk / Non-myopia group (SE > -0.5 D): N = 2,437
    - Never group
    - Tardily group
    - Timely group
  - Myopia group (SE <= -0.5 D): N = 3,074
    - Never group
    - Tardily group
    - Timely group

**Key Finding:** Of the initial 14,572 screened children, 5,511 (37.8% of screened, 93.1% of referred) met inclusion criteria for the final cohort.

## Figure 2: Line Graphs Showing Cumulative Incidence and SE Progression

**Caption:** "Fig. 2 Line graphs showing the cumulative incidence for myopia among children with high risk (A); Mean SE change in all children (B), children with high risk (C) and children with myopia (D) during the 6 follow-up years by referral compliance."

**Type:** Multi-panel figure with 4 panels (A-D)

### Panel A: Cumulative Incidence of Myopia (Kaplan-Meier Survival Curves)
- **Type:** Kaplan-Meier survival curve
- Shows cumulative incidence of myopia over six years in the High-risk group.
- Three curves: Never (blue), Tardily (red), Timely (green).
- X-axis: follow-up time in years (0-6).
- Y-axis: probability of remaining non-myopic (0-1.0).
- Number at risk tables shown below the curves.
- Curves begin to separate after approximately 2 years and diverge further over time.
- **Key findings:**
  - Timely group: lowest cumulative incidence (57.14%), highest survival probability
  - Tardily group: highest cumulative incidence (69.31%), lowest survival probability
  - Never group: intermediate (64.76%)
  - Median survival: Never=4.92 yrs, Tardily=4.50 yrs, Timely=5.83 yrs
  - Log-rank test P < 0.001

### Panel B: Mean SE Change in All Children
- **Type:** GAM-based curves with confidence intervals
- Annual SE change curves over 6 follow-up years.
- X-axis: age in years (~5-11).
- Y-axis: annual SE change in diopters.
- Shaded areas represent 95% CIs.
- **Key findings:**
  - Timely (green): +0.07 +/- 0.60 D/year (slowest progression)
  - Never (blue): -0.06 +/- 0.57 D/year
  - Tardily (red): -0.14 +/- 0.50 D/year (fastest progression)
  - P = 0.005

### Panel C: Mean SE Change in Children with High Risk (Non-Myopia at Baseline)
- **Type:** GAM-based curves with confidence intervals
- Same axis structure and color coding as Panel B.
- **Key findings:**
  - Timely: -0.28 +/- 0.37 D/year
  - Never: -0.32 +/- 0.37 D/year
  - Tardily: -0.36 +/- 0.37 D/year
  - Tardily group shows most rapid myopic shift, particularly between ages 7-9
  - P = 0.006

### Panel D: Mean SE Change in Children with Myopia (Already Myopic at Baseline)
- **Type:** GAM-based curves with confidence intervals
- **Key findings:**
  - Average SE change in myopia group: -0.08 +/- 0.55 D/year
  - Stratified by Never, Tardily, and Timely groups
  - Protective effect of timely referral becomes more apparent with increasing age
