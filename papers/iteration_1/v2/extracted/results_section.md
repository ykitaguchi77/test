# Extracted Results Section

## Baseline Characteristics

- Total cohort: 5,511 preschool children who received a referral recommendation
- Mean age: 5.25 +/- 0.75 years
- Sex: 52.24% male (2,879 boys)
- Mean baseline SE: -0.87 +/- 1.29 D
- Prevalence at baseline: 35.08% of children aged 4-6 years exhibited visual abnormalities
  - 13.98% were in high-risk groups (Non-myopia, SE > -0.5 D with visual abnormalities)
  - 21.1% already had myopia (SE <= -0.5 D)
- High-risk / Non-myopia group: N = 2,437
- Myopia group: N = 3,074

## Referral Compliance

- Of 5,511 children who received a referral recommendation, 1,327 (24.08%) sought clinical services at an ophthalmic hospital at least once during follow-up.
- Timely referral compliance: only 2.03% (112/5,511) sought services within 3 months.
- Never group: N = 4,184 (75.92%)
- Tardily group: N = 1,215 (22.05%)
- Timely group: N = 112 (2.03%)
- No significant difference in compliance rates between High-risk and Myopia groups (chi-square = 0.14, P = 0.71).

## Follow-up

- Among children at high risk for myopia, mean follow-up: 5.00 +/- 0.94 years
- Mean number of examinations: 4.17 +/- 1.25

## Myopia Incidence (High-Risk Group)

- After six years of follow-up, 65.53% of children developed myopia.
- Total myopia incidence events: 1,597 over 12,185 person-years.
- Overall incidence rate: 131.06 per 1,000 person-years.

### Cumulative Incidence by Referral Compliance Group:
- Never group: 64.76%
- Tardily group: 69.31%
- Timely group: 57.14%
- Log-rank test P < 0.001

### Incidence Rate per 1,000 Person-Years by Referral Compliance Group:
- Never group: 129.53
- Tardily group: 138.34
- Timely group: 118.42

## Survival Analysis

### Median Survival Times (time to myopia onset):
- Never group: 4.92 years (95% CI: 4.83-4.92)
- Tardily group: 4.50 years (95% CI: 4.00-4.92)
- Timely group: 5.83 years (95% CI: 4.42-5.92)

## Cox Proportional Hazard Model Results

### Unadjusted Hazard Ratios (vs Never):
- Tardily group: HR = 1.31 (95% CI: 1.10-1.55; P < 0.001)
- Timely group: HR = 0.67 (95% CI: ~0.33-0.93; P < 0.05)

### Adjusted Hazard Ratios (adjusted for age, sex, and SE at study entry):
- Tardily group: HR = 1.31 (95% CI: 1.10-1.55; P < 0.001)
- Timely group: HR = 0.55 (95% CI: 0.33-0.93; P = 0.026)

### Other Covariates in Adjusted Model:
- Age (per year): HR = 1.08 (95% CI: 1.02-1.15; P = 0.012)
- Sex (male vs female): HR = 0.95 (95% CI: 0.86-1.05; P = 0.324)
- Baseline SE (per D): HR = 0.72 (95% CI: 0.68-0.76; P < 0.001)
- District (Minhang vs Jing'an): HR = 1.12 (95% CI: 0.99-1.27; P = 0.078)
- District (Pudong vs Jing'an): HR = 1.05 (95% CI: 0.93-1.19; P = 0.432)

## SE Progression Results (GAM Analysis)

### All Children Combined -- Annual SE Change by Referral Compliance:
- Never group: -0.06 +/- 0.57 D/year
- Tardily group: -0.14 +/- 0.50 D/year
- Timely group: +0.07 +/- 0.60 D/year
- P = 0.005

### High-Risk Group (Non-Myopia at Baseline) -- Annual SE Change:
- Overall: -0.33 +/- 0.37 D/year
- Never group: -0.32 +/- 0.37 D/year
- Tardily group: -0.36 +/- 0.37 D/year
- Timely group: -0.28 +/- 0.37 D/year
- P = 0.006

### Myopia Group (Already Myopic at Baseline) -- Annual SE Change:
- Overall: -0.08 +/- 0.55 D/year

### Overall Timely vs Others:
- P < 0.001

## Subgroup Analyses

- Subgroup analyses stratified by sex showed similar patterns for both boys and girls.
- Sex interaction: P = 0.482 (not significant)
- District interaction: P = 0.712 (not significant)
- District-specific HRs (Timely vs Never):
  - Jing'an: HR = 0.48 (95% CI: 0.24-0.97)
  - Minhang: HR = 0.62 (95% CI: 0.31-1.24)
  - Pudong: HR = 0.58 (95% CI: 0.27-1.25)
