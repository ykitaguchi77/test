- Participant flow (42 enrolled, 2 lost, 20/group analyzed)

- Study design, randomization, blinding details
- Inclusion/exclusion criteria

- Statistical methods (all tests, thresholds, software versions)

**Missing:**
1. **Literature review in Discussion**: The original contains extensive comparisons with 8+ named studies (Epitropoulos, Mulder, Moon, Siddireddy x2, Epstein, Mohammad-Rabei, Murphy, Ballesteros-Sanchez). The draft contains zero named study comparisons. This is an extraction-limited gap (these studies were not in the extracted bundle), but it significantly reduces Discussion depth.
2. **Differential effects of exfoliation techniques**: The original discusses micro-sponge vs. diamond bur exfoliation and their differential mechanisms (bacterial load reduction vs. meibomian gland secretion improvement). The draft does not cover this.
3. **Demodex discussion**: The original discusses Demodex follicularis colonization and BlephEx treatment (Epstein et al., 2020). This is absent from the draft.
4. **Chalazion sub-analysis**: The original references a study on BlephEx for chalazion (2022 RCT). Not in draft.
5. **OSDI as outcome measure justification**: The original notes that "the OSDI is a key indicator of clinical effectiveness" and that the study did not assess microorganism load, lipase activity, or meibomian gland secretion quantity. The draft does not address these unmeasured variables.
6. **Screening/recruitment process**: The original describes the detailed screening workflow (outpatient clinic screening, study team contact, consent form process). The draft summarizes this more briefly.
7. **Basic therapy protocol for new disease occurrences**: The original notes that for new disease, basic therapy (4 weeks lid care + lubricating drops) was recommended first before study inclusion. This detail is absent.
8. **Figure references**: The draft does not reference Figure 1 (treatment photograph) or Figure 2 (CONSORT flowchart) in the text.
9. **Oral contraception and conjunctivitis rows**: Both had 0/0 values in the original Table 1 but are omitted from the draft Table 1.
